# Gary Springer's Garden

Planting trees. Seeing what grows.

A collection of frameworks for relationships, recovery, and the life of faith — grounded in Scripture, informed by neuroscience, and offered freely.

**Live site:** https://syncshepherd-main.github.io/garys-garden/

## Contents

- **The 7-Tier Relational & Ideological Framework** — A hierarchical model of relational and ideological ordering, from individual relationship with God at the root, outward through scriptural community, and into the realm of ideas, discernment, and darkness.
- **A Holistic Model of Recovery & Restoration** — Eight integrated layers mapping the FASTER Scale, brain science, voice of the heart, core needs, and practical boundaries against biblical anthropology.
- **Biblical Roots of the Seven-Layer Model** — A theological supplement tracing each layer's scriptural foundation.
